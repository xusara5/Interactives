A look into Super Bowl LIX

Super Bowl LIX is scheduled for Sunday, Feb. 9, at the Caesars Superdome in New Orleans, LA, with Taylor Swift's boyfriend Travis Kelce playing for the Kansas City Chiefs against the Philadephia Eagles.

YouTube Embed

There's a lot of buzz in the media in anticipation to Kendrick Lamar's Halftime Show. Kendrick Lamar just won the Record and Song of the Year at the Grammys for "Not Like Us," which he will also be performing at the Super Bowl Halftime Show. Apple Music revealed that he will be headlining with guest artist SZA.


I embedded Apple Music's teaser for Kendrick Lamar's halftime show announcing guest artist SZA. It supports the story by letting the readers see for themselves what the reveal looked like and build excitement. Embedding this video was pretty simple, but I noticed that some videos were restricted by the author (like NFL) to be embedded onto other websites, so I tried to find something that would be easy to view right on the page.

Story Map

Curious about where past Super Bowls were held? Take a look at this map to see Super Bowl locations from the past five years.


I used the Knight Lab Story Maps tool to create a map of the stadiums where the Super Bowl was hosted in the past five years. Clicking through the map is an interactive way for readers to see the past stadiums and where across the country they were hosted. The tool was pretty simple to use, but I found that the location search function did not work for any of the stadiums, and I had to use the coordinates to show the place on the map. The coordinate function worked well but it was very specific with syntax. The photos were also a little tricky in terms of sizing; the photos for the last new slides were a too large, but it wasn't clear what the ideal dimensions are.
